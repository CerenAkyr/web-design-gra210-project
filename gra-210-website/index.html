RoboCon

RoboCon is an AI conference focused on the robotics field. It allows the participants to examine how robotics evolved and how to develop it further. The event's goal is to show perspective on how to train robots in terms of completing tasks and understanding humans while considering ethical concerns.

Please note that this event is closed, and attendance is limited to 200 attendees. Register now before we are all booked up. Join us on January 20-21, 2024 (Saturday, Sunday). From 10:00 to 17:30, Bilkent University, Mithat Çoruh Conference Hall.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What can participants expect to gain from attending?

Participants can expect insightful keynote presentations, interactive panel discussions, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities. The conference aims to provide a platform for learning, collaboration, and inspiration in the rapidly evolving AI landscape.

How can I register for RoboCon, what are the registration fees?

To register for RoboCon, please visit our official conference website. The registration process is simple and can be completed online. The event is completely free!

Is lunch provided, are there any specific dietary accommodations available?

Yes, lunch will be provided. Please let us know during the registration process if you have any specific dietary requirements. Our catering team will make every effort to accommodate your needs.
